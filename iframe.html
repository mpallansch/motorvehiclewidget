Motor Vehicle Prioritizing Interventions and Cost Calculator for States (MVPICCS)

MV PICCS is a free interactive calculator that estimates the costs of implementing up to 14 motor vehicle evidence-based strategies, while taking into account a state's specific budget. It also provides state-level estimates of the number of injuries prevented, lives saved, and costs averted from preventing injuries and deaths. The strategies included in MV PICCS are:

  • Primary enforcement of seat belt laws
  • High-visibility enforcement for seat belts and child restraint and booster laws
  • Increased fines for seat belt use
  • Sobriety checkpoints
  • Alcohol interlocks
  • Saturation patrols
  • Universal motorcycle helmet laws
  • Vehicle Impoundment
  • Automated red-light enforcement
  • Automated speed-camera enforcement
  • In person license renewal for adults aged 70 and older
  • Bicycle helmet laws for children
  • License plate impoundment
  • Limits on diversion and plea agreements
